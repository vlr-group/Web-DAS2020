INTRODUCTION

DAS 2020 is the 14th edition of the IAPR sponsored workshop focusing on system-level issues and approaches in document analysis and recognition.The workshop comprises invited speaker presentations, oral and poster presentations, tutorial and working group discussions. Proceedings (regular papers) will be published by Springer.

DAS 2020 will be hosted by Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST) which is in Wuhan, China on July 26-29, 2020. Wuhan, capital of Hubei Province in central China, is a city with both an ancient history and a triving present. As for HUST, it is a comprehensive and multi-disciplinary research university, which is among the first Universities joining the national “211 Project” and “985 Project”.

DAS 2020 will include oral presentations, posters of reaserch or prototype systems. In addition, there will be three keynote speeches addressing key techniques of Document Analysis. Tutorials will be organized as well. The tutorials will be held on the day before the three-day main workshop.
