Venue and Transportation

Due to concerns about COVID-19, DAS 2020 will be held as a fully virtual conference. Nevertheless, all participants are welcomed to visit Wuhan in the future. The following information on venue and transportation is kept for your convenience in future travels.

Conference Venue

The DAS 2020 is proposed to be held at Lytown Hotel , which is a comprehensive 4-star hotel, located in Wuchang District, Wuhan, next to the campus of Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Location and Traffic

The Lytown Hotel is located on Guanggu Avenue, the center of Optics Valley Square, enjoying easy access to the traffic network. And it will take you only 6 minutes drive after exiting from Wuhan Metro at Optics Valley Square station.

  • From Wuchang Railway Station to the hotel (12.8 kilometers)
    • 30 minutes by taxi. The cost takes about 35-45 Yuan (5-7 Dollars).
    • 57 minutes by taking BRT No.1 bus, getting off at Guanggu Avenue, and walking along Guanggu Avenue to arrive.
  • From Wuhan Railway Station (high speed train station) to the hotel (16.5 kilometers)
    • 22 minutes by taxi. The cost takes about 35-45 Yuan (5-7 Dollars).
    • 1 hour and 20 minutes by Wuhan Metro Line 4, and interchange to Line 2 at Zhongnan Road Station, and exit the metro at Optics Valley Square station, and then take the bus 738/591/786 to arrive (getting off at Changfei Fiber station).
  • From Hankou Railway Station (27.1 kilometers)
    • 35 minutes by taxi. The cost takes about 75-85 Yuan (11-12.5 Dollars).
    • 1 hour by Wuhan Metro Line 2 and exit the metro at Optics Valley Square station, and then take the bus 738/591/786 to arrive (getting off at Changfei Fiber station).
  • From Wuhan Tianhe International Airport (47.4 kilometers)
    • 50 minutes by taxi. The cost takes about 150-160 Yuan (22-23.5 Dollars).
    • 1 hour and 45 minutes by Wuhan Metro Line 2 and exit the metro at Optics Valley Square station, and then take the bus 738/591/786 to arrive (getting off at Changfei Fiber station).

Conference Facilities

The Lytown Hotel provides conference rooms of different sizes. The largest conference room could seat 450 people. Each conference room is equipped with first-class facilities, such as LED display screen, projector, wireless microphone, audio, live video broadcast, multifunctional stage, and so on.

Following the previous DAS workshops, the DAS 20 schedules single track for oral presentation. We estimate that the DAS 2020 will accept about 100 papers, including about 30 oral papers and 70 poster papers. However, we can prepare two meeting rooms for possibly two parallel tracks of oral sessions. We will use two meeting rooms, one for 200 attendees (plenary session and parallel session) and one for 100 attendees (parallel session only).  The Caiyue Hall and Fuli Hall satisfy such requirements. For poster sessions, poster boards can be placed in the two sides of Caiyue Hall.
