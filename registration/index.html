Registration

The registration for participating DAS 2020 is free. Anyone interested in Document Analysis and System is welcome. The program of the workshop includes three invited keynote speeches, oral and poster presentations for accepted papers, and discussion groups. In discussion groups, the participants will be partitioned into several groups to discuss the status and prospects of different topics of document analysis.

 

Registration types

DAS2020 provides two types of registration for different participants.

  1. All the authors of accepted papers and the committee members are invited to register. Please be aware that each accepted paper MUST have at least one author to register, to guarantee that the paper is presented at the workshop and included in the proceedings. More authors are welcome to register as well.

          Registration Link: https://das2020reg.vlrlab.net/invite

          Registration due: June 20, 2020 (for regular papers)

                                            July 8, 2020 (for short papers)

  1. For other people who want to participate, registration is accepted based on application-and-approval, so as to control the total number of participants. Approval is mainly based on whether the applicant is working in the document analysis field or not.

          Registration Link: https://das2020reg.vlrlab.net

          Registration due: July 20, 2020.
