May. 16, 2020

NEWS: Owing to concerns about COVID-19, DAS 2020 will be a fully virtual conference to be held from July 26 to July 29, 2020.

Thank you for your submissions to the 14th International Workshop on Document Analysis Systems (DAS 2020), originally planned to be held in Wuhan, China, on May 17-20. From January 2020, the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) has been spreading over the world, first from Wuhan to other cities of China, and now getting serious in other regions in the world. COVID-19 is a global disease, its spreading in any region of our community will affect the organization of international conference. To avoid the risk of being infected with COVID-19, DAS 2020 will be held as a fully virtual conference from July 26 to July 29, 2020. Both oral and poster papers as well as short papers will be presented virtually. The exact virtual conference dates will be informed soon.
